Multiple Images Per Product - Odoo 9.0

Use this module to upload and manage your product photos/images, publish them on your e-commerce website, magnify them in a fancy way for your clients to see, enjoy and make decisions faster and easily

Ability to upload Unlimited number of product Images

You will be able to upload as many photos as you can, arrange them in the order in which you want them to appear on website, publish them on website, give them descriptive names for your clients to see. The photos are uploaded from the product main form making it easier to access and manage them per product. The user is able to see all the photos and their description, sequence number and publishing status in an intuitive kanban view with the image thumb-nails as shown in screenshot below.

Ability to zoom/magnify the images in several different fancy styles

The user has all the freedom and choice to pick the best style to use to magnify the image for the clients to have a closer and clearer look at your product. Depending on your nature of business or your size of pictures, you will choose the best zooming style to use which will make it easier and more fancy for your customers to use your site and have a memorable shopping experience. A few of the styles have been listed below:

  • You can zoom your image in three different ways:
    • Using a zoom window which pops up when you place the mouse cursor on the product image
    • Using a Magnifying lens which pops up when you plae your mouse pointer on the product image
    • Using an inner window within then product images borders
  • Optional ability to use the mousewheel to increase/reduce the magnification is also available in the settings.

Image Zoom Settings

The user will configure the zoom style settings per product. These settings will apply to all images for that particular product
